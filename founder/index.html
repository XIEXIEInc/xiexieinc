About the Founder

Shengzhe Wang is the Founder of XIEXIE Inc. , a company built around the belief that people should have easier access to information, resources, and meaningful connections.

His professional journey has involved international business, cross-cultural communication, marketing strategy, sustainable development, and global innovation initiatives.

Through experiences across different countries and industries, he developed a strong interest in connecting people, ideas, and opportunities.

Why Shengzhe Created XIEXIE Inc.

During his journey working with international companies, universities, and global communities, Shengzhe Wang discovered a common challenge:

Many people have questions, ideas, and goals, but they often do not know where to find the right resources, guidance, or connections.

XIEXIE Inc. was created to become a bridge between people and possibilities.

"When You Don't Know Where to Start, Start with XIEXIE."
