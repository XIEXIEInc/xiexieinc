About the Founder

Shengzhe Wang is the Founder of XIEXIE Inc., a company inspired by the idea that everyone should have access to the right information, connections, and opportunities when they need them.

His professional journey has crossed multiple countries, industries, and cultures, including international business operations, cross-border cooperation, marketing strategy, sustainable development, and global innovation initiatives.

Why I Created XIEXIE Inc.

During my experience working with international companies, universities, and global organizations, I discovered a common challenge:

Many people have ideas, questions, and ambitions, but they often do not know where to find the right resources, guidance, or connections.

XIEXIE Inc. was created to become a bridge between people and possibilities.

Our belief is simple:

When You Don't Know Where to Start, Start with XIEXIE.
